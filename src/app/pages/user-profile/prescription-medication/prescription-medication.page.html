Please be advised that Hort Enterprises has a zero tolerance policy in relation to drugs and alcohol. Employees may at times, be required to undergo random drug and alcohol screens/tests, both within the Company’s site[s] and at various Customer sites. It is a mandatory requirement of site inductions that employees be tested for drugs and/or alcohol consumption before being allowed to work on the site. In relation to prescription medications (and some “over the counter” medications), the Company asks that employees inform their Supervisor of any medication[s] being taken as soon as practicable following the commencement of the medication. This is due to various prescription medications containing substances such as codeine, pseudoephedrine etc., which will be detected as illegal substances when the employee is tested. The taking of prescription medication is not discouraged. The Company merely asks that we be informed of any prescription medication taken so that we and/or the Customer’s site supervision can best manage the effects that may be caused by the medication. Confidentiality of any medical information will be assured. If the employee’s Supervisor is unavailable, the employee is advised to contact Reception on (02) 6391 4100 and inform them of the medication taken. Should an employee fail a drug and/or alcohol screen, they may be subject to disciplinary action as outlined in the Company’s Drug and Alcohol Policy.
